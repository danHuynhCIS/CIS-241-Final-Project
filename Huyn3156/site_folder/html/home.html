About Me

Hi everyone, my name is Dan Huynh, I was born in Kirkland Washington where then moved to Lynnwood where currently I'm residing. I'm the youngest out of two, my sister being the oldest by only two years. Currently, here at Edmonds CC trying to get an Associate's in Computer Science before hopefully transferring to UW Seattle or UW Bothell to complete my education. Growing up I was always around technology, after my uncle immigrated from Vietnam in my early years of life, I've always seen him fiddle around with circuit boards, soldering, computers, and the list goes on. He started at an early age of 16-17 when he would do these things and as a kid, I was very intrigued by this.
